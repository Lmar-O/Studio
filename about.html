      About

      I shoot photo
      & video.

      Based in San Antonio, TX. Cars are the main obsession — the kind of shoot where you're chasing golden hour down a back road and hoping the light holds. Streets and landscapes fill everything in between. Branching into video too.

      Who I am

      I picked up a camera because I wanted to document the cars and places I cared about. That turned into something more serious — learning to read light, scout locations, and figure out how to make a photo feel like a moment rather than a record of one.

      Most of my work is automotive, but I shoot portraits, lifestyle, and events too. I like working with people who have a clear idea of what they want and are willing to put in the legwork to get the shot right. The best results come from that kind of collaboration.

      San Antonio is home base, but I'm happy to travel for the right project. Texas first — beyond that, let's talk.

      Automotive

      Cars first.

      Builds, stock cars, meets, rolling shots. Comfortable with natural light, strobes, and long exposures. I'll scout the location beforehand and time the shoot around the light.

      Street & Landscape

      Everything in between.

      Street photography from years of wandering city blocks — Tokyo, San Antonio, wherever I end up. Landscapes when the light is worth stopping for. These shoots are usually unplanned and better for it.

      Video

      Still building.

      Short-form clips and behind-the-scenes for cars and brands. This side of the studio is still growing — reach out if you want to collab on something. Rates are flexible while I build the reel.

      Frequently Asked Questions.

      Still have one? →
      How do I book a session?
      Fill out the contact form with the shoot type, your general idea, and preferred dates. I'll reply within 1–3 business days. Once we've aligned on scope and date, a 50% deposit locks it in — the remaining balance is due before the shoot.
      How many photos will I receive?
      Depends on the package — 8 edited images for an Automotive Mini up to 30 for a Premium session. Every delivered photo is fully edited. I don't hand over unedited raw files or a dump of every frame I took.
      How long until I get my photos?
      Standard turnaround is 5 business days. Some packages include same-week delivery. I'll confirm your exact timeline when we book — I don't rush edits, but I also don't sit on them.
      How will I receive my photos?
      Via a private online gallery link sent to your email. High-res downloads, no watermarks, yours to keep. You can download everything at once or pick individual files.
      Do you travel outside San Antonio?
      Yes. Travel within a reasonable distance of San Antonio is generally worked into the quote. Anything further — other cities, out-of-state — gets billed at cost: mileage or flights, plus accommodations if it's an overnight. Tell me what you're thinking and I'll put together a number.
      Can I request a specific editing style?
      The portfolio is the best indicator of how I edit. If you have reference shots or a specific look in mind, share them when you reach out — I'll tell you whether it's in range. I won't commit to mimicking another photographer's style wholesale, but I can work toward a tone or mood.
      What if I need to reschedule or cancel?
      Reschedules are free up to 48 hours before the shoot — life happens. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the deposit. If the shoot gets cancelled on my end for any reason, you get a full refund or priority rebooking.
      What's the difference between personal and commercial use?
      Personal use covers sharing on social media, printing for yourself, using in a portfolio — anything non-commercial. Commercial use means the images appear in paid advertising, brand campaigns, editorial placements, or are licensed to a third party. Commercial licensing is scoped separately; reach out to discuss usage, timeline, and budget.
      Do you shoot video too?
      Yes — short-form clips and BTS content for cars and brands. This side of the studio is still growing, so I'm taking on projects selectively while I build the reel. Rates are flexible. Reach out with what you're thinking and let's see if it's a fit.
